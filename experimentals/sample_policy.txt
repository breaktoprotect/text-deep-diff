Password policies must enforce a minimum length of 12 characters.

All user accounts must be reviewed quarterly for appropriateness.

Administrative access must require multi-factor authentication (MFA).

Remote desktop access must be disabled unless specifically approved.

Audit logs must be retained for a minimum of 180 days.

Non-interactive service accounts must not be used for interactive login.

Security patches must be applied within 7 days of release.

Antivirus software must be installed and configured to auto-update.
